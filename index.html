How It Works

Simply upload a clear image of a tooth, select the area you want to analyze, and let our AI model provide instant results. The system has been trained on thousands of tooth images to classify caries stages from healthy enamel to advanced decay.

Key Features

NeutraPerio offers advanced AI analysis for accurate detection, real-time results with confidence scores, a user-friendly interface for simple upload and selection, and serves as an educational tool to learn about dental health through AI insights.

What is our objective?

Tooth decay and the decay of the gums supporting the teeth has been a prominent problem among everyone’s oral health, even those who take extreme care of it. For certain conditions, it is treatable and is able to be cured, while other conditions like enamel erosion, are irreversible. Countless individuals, consequently, are left unawarely deteriorating their teeth health until they are stopped by a dentist, with most cases being too late. Our objective is to provide early detection of enamel diseases.

Our Technology

NeutraPerio utilizes state-of-the-art machine learning models trained on extensive datasets of dental imagery. Our AI employs advanced computer vision techniques to analyze tooth morphology, enamel condition, and potential caries development with high precision and reliability.

Privacy & Security

We prioritize your privacy and data security. All image analysis is performed locally in your browser - no images are uploaded to external servers. Your dental health information remains completely private and under your control.
